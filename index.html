About

About Nagaram Christian Association (NCA) The Nagaram Christian Association is a community-focused, faith-centered organization dedicated to fostering unity, support, and spiritual growth among believers in Nagaram. Rooted in Christian values, NCA works to ensure that every family is heard, supported, and uplifted through fellowship, prayer, and meaningful action. Our mission is to create a strong Christian community that stands together in faith, compassion, and service. Through organized gatherings, welfare initiatives, outreach activities, and developmental programs, we strive to reflect Christ’s love in every aspect of our work. Our Vision To build a united Christian community where every member thrives in faith, safety, and dignity while contributing positively to society. Our Mission Strengthen Christian unity Support families in need Provide welfare services Expand gospel outreach Empower youth & community leadership Preserve sacred and community spaces Promote education, healthcare, and environmental care Why NCA Exists We believe in standing together as one family in Christ, ensuring no believer in Nagaram faces challenges alone. Through coordinated support, prayer, and organized action, NCA serves as a platform for community empowerment and spiritual fellowship.
